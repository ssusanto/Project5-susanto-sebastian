ABOUT ME

I am an innovative self-starter and a creative entrepreneur with a passion in both technology and education. This is evident by my ability to create a business when I was 17. I constantly remind myself of the way my father runs his business while making a positive impact on the society through contributing to various charitable causes. Within one year, I donated part of my business profit to fund a small primary school in Jakarta with textbooks. As William Clay Ford Jr. once said, "Creating a strong business and building a better world are not conflicting goals - they are both essential ingredients from long-term success." This is a motto that I live by and I firmly believe that corporate social responsibility is an essential principle that every business should embody and practice.

It has always been my life goal to create or work in an impactful company. With my drive and passion in technology related business fields, I hope to make a difference in the world by actively engaging society and bringing accessible education to children living in underprivileged neighborhoods around the world.
